=======================================================
5 Conventions for Naming Characters in Sci-fi stories. 
=======================================================

.. post:: 2007-11-28
   :author: tychoish

1. Add superfluous consonants, particularly X and Z.
2. Add superfluous vowels, or overuse "y" as a vowel
3. Append numbers to names
4. Insert Mid-Word stops using the apostrophes.
5. Name alien's utterly mundane names like "Jim."
