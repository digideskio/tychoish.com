=================
The Price of Free
=================


Eventually, the Facebooks and Twitters of the world may be dethroned
and replaced by provider-agnostic protocols, in much the same way
AOL is no longer synonymous with email. However, no one is going to
build protocols that threaten their bottom line. So long as "free"
is paid for by surveillance, the Internet will represent a Faustian
bargain for radical social movements.

-- `The Price of
Free <http://www.socialtextjournal.org/periscope/2009/11/the-price-of-free-1.php>`__
