The matrilineal ancestry of Ashkenazi Jews

"close to one-half of Ashkenazi Jews, estimated at 8,000,000 people,
can be traced back to only 4 women carrying distinct mtDNAs that are
virtually absent in other populations,"

(Via Dienekes' Anthropology Blog.)
