=========
Knit Toys
=========

.. post:: 2008-01-09
   :author: tychoish

Hey folks, in case you were really itching to make a knitted toy, say
for the upcoming `Knitty Couture <http://www.knittycouture.com/>`__ toy
knitting project, I've collected a whole mess of patterns and resources
for your knitting pleasure.
